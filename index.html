    An easy to use PMS tailored for co-living…

    …that wasn't built by dust coughing "IT people"

    Kin helps you save time and standardize how you manage your spaces, so you can move faster, scale quicker and improve the experience for your guests.

    Automate

    Billing

    Creates, sends and tracks invoices every month. We even remind your guests if they forget to pay.

    On- and off-boarding

    Create customizable emails that trigger before, during or after a guests stay.

    Payments

    Every invoice gets a unqiue payment link that accepts all the popular credit/debit cards.

    Organize

    Sales funnel

    Time kills deals. Capture leads, and move them quickly through the sales funnel.

    Maintenance tickets

    No balls dropped. Capture maintenance request and manage them through to completion.

    Bookings

    Booking and room management so easy a toddler could do it.

    Monitor

    Availability

    See who will live in what room when, with a visual calendar. Filter by day, week, month, or year.

    Revenue

    See how your cash flow is doing with reports on revenue by time or location.

    Status

    Keep track of the state of all tickets, bookings and payments.
