    About us

    Property management software for co-living spaces

    Our goal is to take the real-estate industry out of the stone age.

    After staying in co-living spaces across the world, we loved it and wanted to get involved. After some digging, we realized a lot of co-living companies wanted to take advantage of technology to improve their operations and guest experience, but few had the time or resources to dedicate to it.

    That's when we decided to build Kin.

    We quickly realized that the real estate industry as a whole had a very slow technology adoption and saw the opportunity to make an impact.
